About Us:

The Phatty Cloudz Leadaboadz is a revolutionary take on competitive vaping. Vapers from around the world can come together as a community and compare their phatty cloudz with each other. We provide leaderboards for various regions of the world. Not only that but we have also accounted for various conditions that may affect the measurements of phatty cloudz. Our innovative Phatty Cloudz Algorithm was created in September 2017 that allows us to give each phatty cloud a Phattys Rating. This Phattys Ratting is scaled from 1-10. 10 representing a world-class phatty cloud and 1 representing a below average cloud not even worth mentioning. Lets all come together and put aside our differences to make one Phatty Cloud!
